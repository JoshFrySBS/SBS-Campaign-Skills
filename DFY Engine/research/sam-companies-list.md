## Selection criteria (do not skip)

A company qualifies as a research target only if:
- **Size:** 10–80 employees on LinkedIn
- **Sales team:** ≥2 BD/SDR/AE titles visible on LinkedIn
- **Industry:** B2B services (recruitment, SaaS, IT/MSP, AI startup, consulting, MedTech, industrial supply)
- **Geo:** UK-primary (US companies acceptable but lower priority — Companies House signal won't apply)
- **Tooling:** sparse outbound stack visible (no Apollo/Clay/Outreach detected — or unclear)

If a candidate fails any of these, skip and pick another.

**Avoid for the research set:** big companies (>100), pure agencies, non-B2B, anything with obvious enterprise sales tech.
